Catch the Runaway

  • High Concept: A policeman trying to catch the runaway prisoner.
  • Player Experience Goals: Tension to avoid the spikes on the floor and to not fall down and get bites from the mice.
  • Genre: Platformer.
  • Game play: The player moves and jumps with arrow keys. The player should not fall down from the platforms nor touch the spikes. Also, the player should avoid the mice.
  • Prototype goal: The game mechanices are camera movements, jumping and moving.
  • Features: Scary theme song with the fear of spikes and mice.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is a jail with a lot of mice and traps everywhere. And there is a runaway prisoner and a policeman is trying to catch him.
  • Story: A prisoner ran away and a policeman is trying to catch him.
  • Target Audience: Any age with capability to control.
  • Play time: Less than 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: The player should be careful when making jumps and watch out for the wild mice.
